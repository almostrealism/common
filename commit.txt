Fix LICM recursive scope traversal and raise CSE extraction limit

Enable unconditional loop-invariant code motion (LICM) in Repeated.java
by removing the AR_LOOP_INVARIANT_HOISTING environment variable toggle.
LICM Phase 3 now recursively traverses grandchild scopes to find and
hoist invariant declarations, fixing the critical issue where f_assignment
declarations in nested scopes were not being hoisted out of the loop.

Raise ScopeSettings.getMaximumReplacements() from 12 to 48 to support
CSE extraction of genome-only sub-expressions from envelope accumulate
lines (12 envelopes x 3 sub-expressions = 36 extractions needed).

Add comprehensive structural tests for LICM covering grandchild hoisting,
dependency chain variance, transitive invariant chains, mixed
invariant/variant declarations, and non-declaration filtering.
